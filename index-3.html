Let’s Answer the questions?

Answers to Your Most Frequently Asked Questions

PrivacyCrumb is a cookie consent management platform that helps websites comply with global privacy regulations like GDPR, CCPA, and CPRA. It automatically scans your website for cookies, categorizes them, generates a compliant cookie banner, and manages user consent preferences — all with a simple code snippet you add to your site.
Yes, PrivacyCrumb is fully compliant with GDPR (EU & UK), CCPA (California), CPRA, and other major privacy regulations worldwide. Our platform automatically adapts the consent banner and cookie handling based on the visitor's location, ensuring your website meets legal requirements in every jurisdiction.
Our free cookie scanner crawls your website and detects all cookies and trackers being used. It then classifies them into categories such as Necessary, Analytics, Marketing, and Functional. You'll receive a detailed report showing which cookies are compliant and which ones need attention — all without needing to sign up.
Installation is simple — just copy and paste a small JavaScript snippet into the head section of your website. PrivacyCrumb also provides dedicated plugins for popular platforms like WordPress, Shopify, and Wix, making integration even easier. The entire setup takes less than 5 minutes.
PrivacyCrumb automatically classifies cookies into four main categories: Necessary (essential for site functionality), Analytics (tracking and performance), Marketing (advertising and retargeting), and Functional (enhanced features and preferences). You can also create custom categories to match your specific needs.
Absolutely! PrivacyCrumb offers full customization of your cookie consent banner. You can adjust colors, fonts, layout, button text, and position to match your brand identity perfectly. Choose from pre-built templates or design your own banner from scratch using our visual editor.
Yes, PrivacyCrumb includes free generators for both Privacy Policies and Cookie Policies. These tools create legally-compliant documents tailored to your website's specific cookie usage and data practices. The generated policies are automatically kept up to date as your cookie usage changes.
Yes, PrivacyCrumb offers a generous free plan that includes cookie scanning, a basic consent banner, and essential compliance features. For websites that need advanced features like detailed analytics, custom branding, multi-language support, and priority support, we offer affordable paid plans to fit every budget.
